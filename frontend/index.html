PIX is Brazil's national instant payment network — bigger than Venmo, CashApp, and Zelle combined. Every payment settles in under 3 seconds. PIX Oracle Settlement bridges this infrastructure to Cardano: lock ADA in a smart contract, confirm a PIX payment, and the Charli3 Pull Oracle guarantees the exchange rate at the exact moment of settlement.
